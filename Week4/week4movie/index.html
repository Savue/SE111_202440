Baldur's Gate 3 is a turn-based strategy RPG based in the Dungeons & Dragons world, you need to gather your party, meet intresting characters, make hard decisions and sacrifices, fight to survive in lands of the unknown. Mysterious powers are awakening inside you and party members, drawn from a alien parasite planted in your brain. Resist it or take the power and turn darkness against itself.
